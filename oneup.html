Innovation has always been at the heart of the J.R. Simplot Company. With OneUP™ liquid fertilizer, that commitment to constant improvement has been brought to a whole new level.

No other liquid fertilizer on the market combines the same advanced technologies. OneUP™ is both a plant stimulant and soil builder, rich in low salt liquid and featuring advanced photosynthesis and nutrient uptake technologies designed to help producers boost yields -- and profitability.

  • Product includes no bacteria
